## 들여보기

> A chatbot can provide comfort for the truly isolated. But loneliness is more than just pain

> ... rather than recoiling in horror, we ought to consider what A.I. companions could offer to those who are lonely.

> In the social sciences and the humanities, A.I. tends to be greeted less as a technological advance than as **a harbinger of decline.**

>  But what really rankles is the idea that these digital interlocutors are a plausible substitute for real friends or family.

> What’s undeniable is that **loneliness is now being taken seriously enough to warrant government intervention**—both Japan and the U.K. have appointed ministers for loneliness.

## 내어보기

- 피할수 없을 만큼 이미 사회 전반으로 챗봇이 자리잡고 있다
